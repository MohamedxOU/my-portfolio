## Overview

WheelMeet is a social platform designed for automotive enthusiasts to showcase their vehicles, discover local events, and connect with like-minded individuals. Built with Flutter for cross-platform compatibility and powered by Supabase for real-time functionality.

### Frontend
- **Flutter**: Single codebase for iOS and Android
